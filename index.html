Welcome

Thuthuka Primary School is a highly respected and well-known school in the town of Thuthukela, Southern Province, Eastern Cape. Established in 1980, it offers a comprehensive curriculum that caters to the diverse needs of its students, ensuring that every child is enriched and equipped to succeed in their lives.


The Principal and staff of Thuthuka Primary are committed to the pursuit of excellence. We believe in quality, progressive education which aims at the development of responsible young people who are fully equipped for success: academically, physically, financially, socially, spiritually and morally.
